Our Story

Driving the future of Sri Lankan logistics with integrity, innovation, and unwavering commitment.

Who We Are

Ceylon Prime Logistics was founded to bridge the gap between efficiency and reliability in the Sri Lankan transportation sector. Recognizing the critical role that logistics plays in our island nation's economy, we established a company built on the backbone of a modern prime mover fleet and a team of seasoned experts.

Based in the heart of Colombo, close to the port and major economic centers, we are strategically positioned to handle import and export cargo with speed. Our commitment goes beyond just transport; we provide peace of mind.

Our Vision

To be the undisputed leader in Sri Lanka's logistics industry, setting benchmarks for safety, technology integration, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To deliver excellence in every mile we cover. We strive to empower businesses by providing robust, scalable, and efficient supply chain solutions.
